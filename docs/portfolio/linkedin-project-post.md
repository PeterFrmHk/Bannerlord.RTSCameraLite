I’m sharing a **work-in-progress** personal project: **RTS Commander Doctrine**, a *Mount & Blade II: Bannerlord* mod that experiments with a **commander-first** battle layer on top of TaleWorlds’ native systems.

The focus isn’t “replace Bannerlord” — it’s **clarity and discipline**: explicit commander mode, doctrine and eligibility modeled as data, rally and spacing planning, and cavalry workflows described as stateful doctrine with **careful** boundaries around native orders (some paths stay off until build-specific research is satisfied).

Technically, it’s been a great exercise in **MissionView-driven orchestration**, adapter-style isolation for risky engine calls, throttled scans instead of hot-loop spam, and **portfolio-grade** diagnostics so design intent is visible in logs and captures — not only in code.
